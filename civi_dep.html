Overview

While living in a time of technological advancement and IT revolution, the field of civil engineering nevertheless adheres to its historical foundations. This area of engineering deals with all of society’s and the general public’s needs, such as water and shelter, bridges and roads, dams and tunnels, environmental pollution, power plants, and other industrial projects. As long as society continues to expand and develop, the civil engineering profession will continue.

We provide intensive training, internships, expert talks, site visits, etc to make the industry ready.

Vision


To be a Centre of excellence for imparting quality education in Civil Engineering encompassing Ethical Environmental and Economical aspects of the Society.

Mission


To provide highly competent Civil Engineers to the society and industry with effective teaching and research experience.
